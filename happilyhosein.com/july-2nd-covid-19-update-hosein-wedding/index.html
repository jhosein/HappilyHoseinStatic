July 2, 2020 in Announcements

Nashville COVID-19: Impact on guest count

Unfortunately, Nashville rolled back its reopening plan for COVID-19 limiting our wedding to just 25 guests. This has forced us to make the difficult decision to limit our guests to just our wedding party and close family. This will be our new plan going forward unless the situation changes allowing more guests to attend.

Please continue to RSVP and we will let you know at least 2 weeks prior if we can allow more guests.
